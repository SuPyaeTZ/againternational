Management / Consultancy

AGA International Private Limited by itself and its group of international team of associates provides multi-disciplinary expertise in

  • Business Development
  • Project Development
  • Consultancy
  • Project Management
  • Architectural Planning and
  • Interior Design

Projects that the group have been involved includes town and city planning, mega malls shopping design consultancy and project management, hotels, industrial parks, upgrading projects, conservation, developing of franchising business and developing of new business potentials.

The team have a combined and consolidated experiences in excess of 30 years both locally and internationally. The group goes where investment opportunities call. They include the fast growing economy of China, India, Malaysia and Indonesia to the emerging economy of Cambodia and Mongolia.

This enable the group to expands its network and thus expertise to offer professional services to serve its base of associates and clients on an international scale.

About

AGA International Private Limited was established as an architectural construction, management consultancy firm involved with handling architecture investments and advise, regional city and town planning, construction administration and management.Since the early eighties, its key personnels have gone regional with the tie-up of foreign departments both in Singapore and overseas to study the investment opportunities and fast growing development in places like The People's Republic of China and elsewhere.
