Company Profile

Profile

AGA International Private Limited was established as an architectural construction, management consultancy firm involved with handling architecture investments and advise, regional city and town planning, construction administration and management.

Worldwide Practice

Since the early eighties, its key personnels have gone regional with the tie-up of foreign departments both in Singapore and overseas to study the investment opportunities and fast growing development in places like The People's Republic of China and elsewhere. This has enabled the firm to enlarge upon its broader concern of the profession to serve the clients that are operating on an international scale whilst still remaining responsive to the equally important needs of their local commitments in its bold venture. AGA International Private Limited team of professionals' are experienced in managing projects with the required specialists from town and city planning, hotels, shopping complexes, marinas, offices, mixed developments, residential and industrial parks. The regional arm has covered the Chinese cities such as Shanghai, Xian, Qingdao, Dalian, Xiamen, in the other provinces of Shandong, Jiang Su and Fu Jian. In the Middle East in Khattar, Doha and Abu Dhabi. In the region, Singapore, Malaysia, Vietnam and Indonesia.

Philosophy

The firm expands, develops and refines on the aspirations of its associates and partners to maturity. With a constant emphasis on environmental impact, social considerations, integrity of the latest technical application and balance of financial viability. Each conceptual vision is merticulously realised to its authentic completion.

Scope of Service

The firm's positive contribution in its commitment centres on its established team of multi-disciplinary professionals aimed at maintaining a constant continuum in its comprehensive services. Our approach is focused on the complete package involvement from advising our clients on the project feasibilities, conceptualisation to documentation, administration and completion.
