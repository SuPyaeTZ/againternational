Award

AGA INTERNATIONAL PRIVATE LIMITED has been honored with multiple prestigious awards in recognition of our excellence in architectural design and urban development. Our dedication to innovation, aesthetic quality, and functionality has earned us the trust and acclaim of the industry.

  • HDB Design Excellence Award 2000 – Bedok South Road (Batch 3)
  • SIA – ICI Dulux Colour Awards 2001 – Tanglin Halt Green
  • HDB Design Excellence Award 2001 – New Upper Changi Road (Batch 4)
  • SIA Merit Award – For exceptional design quality and contribution to the built environment
  • HDB Design Excellence Award 2011 – Continued excellence in residential planning

These accolades reflect our continuous pursuit of design excellence, client satisfaction, and our commitment to shaping inspiring spaces across Singapore.

About

AGA International Private Limited was established as an architectural construction, management consultancy firm involved with handling architecture investments and advise, regional city and town planning, construction administration and management.Since the early eighties, its key personnels have gone regional with the tie-up of foreign departments both in Singapore and overseas to study the investment opportunities and fast growing development in places like The People's Republic of China and elsewhere.
